SLAAC DATA VIEWER

About

SLAAC DATA VIEWER allows you to search and visualize land parcel data from the Sheema district database.

How it works:

  1. Enter a Parcel ID or Owner Name in the search bar.
  2. Click "Search" to find and zoom to the parcel on the map.
  3. Click on a parcel to view its details.
